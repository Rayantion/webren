FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?

Not at all. Your job is to refer people — we handle all the technical work. If your contact needs a website, just introduce them to us and log the lead in your portal.

When do I get paid?

Commission is tracked monthly through the portal. Once a client's payment is confirmed, your commission is marked and paid out to you. Aaron handles approvals directly.

How long do I earn commission?

For as long as your client is on an active plan with Web人. There is no cap — a client who stays for 3 years means 3 years of commission for you.

Is my spot really free?

Yes — the first 20 agents to register and get approved pay NT$0/month, permanently. After that, new agents pay NT$200/month. Once you're in, you're in for free.

What plans can I refer clients to?

Option A Basic (NT$3,000/mo), Option A Professional (NT$4,500/mo), Option A Premium (NT$5,000/mo), and custom Option B projects. Full pricing is on our Pricing page.
